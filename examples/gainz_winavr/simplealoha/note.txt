simplealoha
This application tests/demonstrates the svc_simplealoha module. The Simple Aloha is an simplified version of Aloha protocol comparing to its standard implementation. The simple version is easy for implementation but the efficiency is pretty low. It's used for teaching only.



Aloha_send & Aloha_receive
========================================================================
1) Project Description: 
this is a program used to test our mac protocl--aloha protocol.

2) Experiments and Notice 实验步骤和实验中的注意事项
使用A,B, C三个节点，A点为发送方，B点为接受方，C点为噪声。
打开A节点和B节点，可以看到发送节点的黄灯闪烁，表示发送出数据且收到返回ACK，表明发送成功。
打开C节点，C节点不断发送数据，加入噪声干扰，我们发现黄灯的闪烁频率基本不变，表明Aloha协议能够保证A、B两节点之间的通信。
